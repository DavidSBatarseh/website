Three Body Gravity Simulation

Three body gravity simulations are known to be chaotic systems, and look really cool. We use processingjs to simulate the gravity between three objects, and crudely autocorrect the center of mass of the system to keep problems with accuracy in line with conservation of momentum. #todo: rewrite to be faster, add tracers.
